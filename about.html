About VRG Power Dash

Innovating smart home energy solutions for a sustainable future

Our Mission

VRG Power Dash is dedicated to revolutionizing home energy management through innovative IoT technology and intelligent automation.

Our system combines voltage stabilization, remote device control, and real-time energy monitoring to create a safer, more efficient, and environmentally friendly home environment.

As a bachelor's thesis project at Universitatea Politehnica Timișoara, VRG Power Dash represents the culmination of extensive research in embedded systems, IoT connectivity, and sustainable energy practices.

What We Offer

  • Voltage Stabilization: Protect your devices from power fluctuations and extend their lifespan.
  • Remote Control: Manage all household appliances from anywhere via web or mobile.
  • Energy Monitoring: Track real-time consumption with detailed analytics.
  • Automated Scheduling: Set up custom schedules to optimize energy usage.
  • Green Technology: Reduce your carbon footprint with intelligent power management.
